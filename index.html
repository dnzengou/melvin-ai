What is a chatbot

A chatbot is an artificial intelligence (AI) powered robot, that can automatically answer most customer inquiries through text, or images. It uses machine learning to both understand what the user is asking for and delivering the correct response, a service or a product.

Why chatbots are awesome

A conversational app is the new channel for the Voice Of Customer. It can be a personal assistant, a customer service rep, or a helpful friend.
I'm everywhere customers are, whether it is on your website, a stand-alone platform or on social networks.
